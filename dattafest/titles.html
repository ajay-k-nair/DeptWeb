Speaker: Bhaskar Bagchi
Title: The uniqueness conjecture
Time: 09:30 am – 10:15 am

Abstract: We shall discuss one of the most famous problems in Combinatorics; the folklore conjecture that for each prime number p, there is a unique projective plane of order p. The focus will be on the coding theoretic approach to this problem.

Speaker: Satoshi Murai
Title: Stackedness, tightness, and commutative algebra
Time: 11:30 am – 12:15 pm (online)

Abstract: Stackedness and tightness are attractive properties of triangulated manifolds, which Basudeb has a lot of contributions to them. These properties are defined from combinatorial and topological point of view, but they actually have a nice connection to commutative algebra theory. In this talk, I will talk how Basudeb's works on stacked and tight triangulations connect to commutative algebra theory.
