Timetable for October 2020-January 2021 term

The following is the schedule for the final examination of the courses for October 2020 - January 2021. The examination date for each course is specified on the basis of the Monday/Tuesday time slot in which the classes were conducted. If the instructor has used a mixed slot such as Monday 2.30-4, and Thursday 10- 11.30, then the first of these decides the examination date. Please note that January 26th. is a holiday, and those exams are to be conducted on January 19th.
