Title

: 

"Fourier coefficients of Siegel modular forms"
Abstract

: 

  We will discuss some questions concerning the existence of families of Siegel modular forms with some prescribed non-zero Fourier 
 coefficients. If time permits, we also plan to discuss the question of characterizing cusp forms by the growth of their Fourier coefficients.
 Both are recent joint works with Siegfried Boecherer.
