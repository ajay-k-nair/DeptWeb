Symposium on Stochastic Control and Applications

On the occasion of the superannuation of Prof. Mrinal Kanti Ghosh

Monday, 25 July, 2022

 

A hybrid symposium on Stochastic Control and Applications will be organized by the Department of Mathematics, Indian Institute of Science on Monday, 25 July, 2022 on the occasion of the superannuation of Prof. Mrinal Kanti Ghosh.

This is part of a three-day symposium on the occasion of superannuation of three of our faculty members.

Invited Speakers

  • Vivek Borkar (IIT-B)
  • Suresh Kumar (IIT-B)
  • B. Rajeev (ISI Bangalore)
  • Anup Biswas (IISER Pune)
  • Imran Biswas (TIFR-CAM)
  • Gopal Basak (ISI, Kolkatta)
